Handmade Marketplace

Artisan

Every Piece Tells a Story

Artisan is a marketplace that gathers talented Syrian craftspeople in one place — from pottery, ceramics and home-made preserves to embroidery, woodwork, Aleppo soap and 3D printing. Makers showcase their work and reach customers across the country, while buyers browse crafts by category, agree with the artisan directly over WhatsApp, and pay cash on delivery. Listing a product takes just three simple steps — if you can post a photo on Instagram, you're ready to sell.
