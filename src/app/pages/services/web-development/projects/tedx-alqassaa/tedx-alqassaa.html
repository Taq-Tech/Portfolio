Events & Community Platform

TEDx AlQassaa

Where Ideas Ignite and Innovations Unfold

The official platform for TEDx AlQassaa — an independently organized TED event held in Damascus, Syria, that brings the community together to share ideas worth spreading. The website showcases events, featured speakers, the organizing team and sponsors, lets attendees verify their attendance and download digital certificates, and publishes a blog and the rules of attending. We also enhanced the CMS (Content Management System) Dashboard so the team can manage events, speakers, certificates and content end-to-end without touching code.

  • Angular
  • Nest.js
  • MongoDB
  • Next.js
