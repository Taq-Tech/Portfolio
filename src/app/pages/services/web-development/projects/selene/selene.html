Desktop Productivity Suite

Selene

Pretty tools that actually help you get things done

Selene is a small suite of cozy productivity apps designed to feel good to use while keeping you focused. It brings together three little tools made with care — Ember, a Pomodoro focus timer with a living pixel-art flame; Lumina, a no-account Kanban task tracker; and Fable, a private journal & notes app. Every app runs fully offline, installs as a lightweight desktop app, and keeps all of your data on your own device.

  • Angular
  • Tauri
  • SQLite

Three Apps, One Suite

Lumina keeps your tasks in order. Ember keeps you focused. Fable keeps your story.

Ember

Focus Timer

A Pomodoro timer that actually feels warm. A pixel-art flame flickers through your focus session — stay in the zone, then breathe.

  • Focus, short break & long break modes
  • Living pixel-art flame that reacts to your session
  • Set your intention — “What are you burning toward?”
  • Works offline — installs as a desktop app
Available now

Lumina

Task Tracker

A task board that stays out of your way. Add tasks, move them across columns, check them off. No accounts, no subscriptions — it all lives on your device.

  • Kanban board — To do, In progress, Done
  • Priority levels with colour coding
  • Archive, templates, and drag to reorder
  • Works offline — installs as a desktop app
Available now

Fable

Journal & Notes

Your private grimoire. Write today's entry, capture fleeting ideas, collect what matters — all in one warm, unhurried space. No folders, no friction.

  • Daily journal with dated entries & a 7-day strip
  • Free-form notes with colour-coded tags
  • Streak counter — keep the habit alive
  • Yours alone — everything stays on your device
