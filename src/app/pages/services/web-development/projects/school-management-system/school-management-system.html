Education Management

School Management System

Multi-role operations platform for educational institutions

A comprehensive Laravel platform that digitizes the daily operations of schools and academies. Four role-tailored dashboards — Administration, Teachers, Students, and Parents — coordinate the full academic, examination, attendance, and financial lifecycle within a fully bilingual workspace that supports both Arabic and English with native right-to-left and left-to-right layouts.

  • Four role-based dashboards purpose-built for administrators, teachers, students, and parents, each surfacing only the views and actions relevant to its audience.
  • Complete academic hierarchy management covering grades, classes, sections, subjects, teachers, and students within a unified institutional directory.
  • Attendance tracking with daily registration, per-section breakdowns, and consolidated reporting for administrators, teachers, and parents.
  • Integrated examination engine with quiz authoring, reusable question banks, automated scoring, and per-student degree records.
  • End-to-end finance module spanning fee schedules, student invoices, receipts, processing charges, and reconciled student and fund accounts.
  • Parent portal that mirrors academic progress and attendance across every enrolled child in a single consolidated view.
  • Live online classes with meeting integration, scheduling, and direct join links for students and teachers.
  • Student promotion workflow with a historical ledger of academic-year transitions.
  • Library catalogue scoped by grade, classroom, section, and assigning teacher.
  • Configurable institution settings — academic session, term boundaries, school identity, contact details, and logo — managed from a single screen.
  • Full bilingual interface with Arabic and English localization and seamless RTL/LTR switching.
  • Laravel Blade
  • MySQL
